Project Idea

For my project idea I have come up with a speedrunning tool that can automatically split the time after completing a segment in a video game which allows the person playing the game to have full concentration of the game without having to manually press a button to split.

This idea is not interesting but useful for people who enjoy speedrunning video games and require full concentration on their game. Having another person to split or stop the timer for the game can result in inaccurate timing corresponding to the run as the person.

The app will automatically split upon detecting a frame from a video capture software that corresponds to the image sampled which signifies that a segment of the game is done. This can vary on a lot of games as to how the segment or level is completed but for this, I will use Mario Kart 8 Deluxe for the app to automatically split time on in which needs to split upon seeing the “finish” sign on screen as soon as I finish a race. Because the app is AI based it needs to detect the same message or screen in game that would signify that the segment is complete multiple times before learning the pattern. The app will have its own timer and profiles for specific games and categories to run.
